Esa Mäkeläinen

CEO & Founder

Education

MSc Economics, Helsinki School of Economics

Before founding Valuatum in 2000, Esa worked across multiple dimensions of financial analysis and valuation: as a management consultant in value-based management at SMCF, Rettig/Marli, and Kamensky Consulting; in credit risk analysis at Merita Bank; and in equity analysis at Nordea Securities. Esa is currently also active as a board member in several IT companies.
