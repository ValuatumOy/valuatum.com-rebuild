Working at Valuatum

Build the tools that drive financial decisions.

Valuatum is a lean, expert team working at the intersection of finance and technology. We build analytical software that financial professionals worldwide rely on for critical decisions. If that kind of work appeals to you, we'd like to hear from you.

We're particularly interested in people with experience or strong interest in company valuation, credit risk analysis, data science, or Java/Excel development — and who can communicate fluently in both Finnish and English.

We welcome both full-time and part-time applications, and are happy to hear from experienced professionals and motivated interns alike.
