Our Story

Built by practitioners,
for practitioners.

Valuatum was founded in 2000 by a team of former equity and credit risk analysts. That practitioner background is not incidental — it shapes every product decision we make. We understand the analytical demands of financial work because we have done it ourselves.

Our areas of expertise span equity valuation models, credit risk analysis, automated reporting, interactive research libraries, and extranet solutions for portfolio managers and private equity investors.

We operate with a Software as a Service model. That means our clients receive not just the platform, but ongoing maintenance and continuous development — keeping their tools sharp as markets and requirements evolve.

What We Deliver

Credit risk and equity research. Both, done right.

Valuatum delivers credit risk and equity research solutions to credit institutions, stockbrokers, investment banks, private equity companies, and asset managers worldwide. We have satisfied sell-side, buy-side, and credit risk customers on several continents.

Our platform is designed to be configurable — adaptable to the workflows, report formats, and data requirements of each client organization, without sacrificing analytical rigor.
